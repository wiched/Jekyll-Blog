About page

Short introduction:

I'm Lubo, a front-end developer and web designer from Sofia, Bulgaria. I decided to share my journey into JavaScript and help others that will be in my position. I've been creating on the web for around 7 years and I have become pretty good at some parts of it. I hang out on Twitter and would love to meet you!

A little more...

I'm super passionate about design, development and system administration. I worked as system and network administrator for one of the biggest film studios, but the love for the web made me change my career path. I spend most of my time working with HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript (ES6/ES2015). I do a lot of WordPress work but I'm trying to become a full stack javascript developer in the next year.

If you came here to find out what theme, apps, monitors or headphones I use, check out my uses page

When not creating websites, I love to spend time with my beautiful girlfriend Ellie, my family and friends. I love reading, and I try to read as much as possible. If you want to see what I'm currently reading you can check my Goodreads profile.
